# Modules

Modules are the **outworld** components of your ant setup — the spaces outside the nest where ants forage, feed, and explore. They are not nesting chambers (that is what the [Formicaria](formicaria.md) are for). Modules connect to each other, to formicaria, and to tubing via the [CV2 connector system](connectors.md).

All module connection faces follow the same 35×35mm grid, so any module can connect to any other module or formicarium — mix and match freely as your colony grows.

## Print Settings

- 0.2mm layer height
- 15% infill
- PLA, PETG, or any hard filament
- Portal feeder lids must be printed in TPU to press-fit correctly

Use a colour that contrasts with your ants so you can spot them easily.

## Portal

![Portal](img/10.jpg)

The Portal is the recommended starting point for a new colony. It acts as the first small outworld: connect your founding test tube here and the ants have a safe space to explore while still living in the tube. It is small enough to not overwhelm a founding colony, and has built-in feeding access so you can provide food and water without disturbing the setup.

As the colony grows, the Portal stays useful — it can remain as a central hub connecting the nest to tubing, outworlds, and other modules.

**Parts to print:**

- 1x Portal body (`CV2_Portal.stl`)
- 1x Portal Lid (`OF_Portal_Lid.stl`) — covers the top to keep ants in and light out
- 2x Portal Feeder Lids (`OF_Portal_FeederLid.stl`) — print in TPU; press-fit into the two feeding holes

**Plexiglass:** 26mm × 62mm × 2mm. Can be press-fit or secured with M3 screws.

**Specs:** 70mm × 40mm × 20mm — 4 connector ports

## 4xGateway

![4xGateway](img/11.jpg)

The 4xGateway is the base expansion unit — a plain open chamber with connector ports on all four sides and a plexiglass viewing panel. Use it to add foraging space, create a junction between multiple tubes, or bridge the Portal to a formicarium. Because it is the same 35×35mm footprint as a single connector face, it is the most compact way to add a new connection point to your layout.

**Plexiglass:** 27mm × 27mm × 2mm. Can be press-fit or secured with M3 screws.

**Specs:** 35mm × 35mm × 20mm — 4 connector ports

---

## 6xGateway

![6xGateway](img/12.jpg)

The 6xGateway is a larger expansion chamber — twice as wide as the 4xGateway, with six connector ports and air vents. Use it when you need more open space for foraging, want to connect more modules in a single hub, or are building a larger layout where the 4xGateway feels cramped. The extra ports also make it a natural central junction for multi-branch setups.

**Plexiglass:** 62mm × 27mm × 2mm. Can be press-fit or secured with M3 screws.

**Specs:** 70mm × 35mm × 20mm — 6 connector ports
